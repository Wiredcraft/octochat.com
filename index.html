Sign in with GitHub

If your team already works on Github, you'll love it.

  • Organize your issues

    Start organizing issues and track their progress by picking labels you want to visualize as lists.

  • Drag & Drop

    Keep your issues organized and up to date by simply dragging them from one list to another.

  • Real-time collaboration

    Your whole team see changes by others in real-time; no need to reload the page to see what's happening.

  • Fast search

    Filter your entire issue queue in a snap; search by keywords and see who's working on what.

  • Multiple repositories

    Coordinate and collaborate over multiple repositories' issues queues for large projects.

  • Video calls

    Call in the entire team for your daily scrum right from the issue queue.
